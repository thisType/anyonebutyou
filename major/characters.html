Main Characters

Ben Bea

Main Characters:

  • Bea (Sydney Sweeney): A quick-witted and independent woman who isn't afraid to speak her mind. After a disastrous first date with Ben, she's determined to avoid him at all costs. However, fate throws them back together in Australia, leading to a hilarious situation where they must pretend to be a couple.
  • Ben (Glen Powell): A charming and confident guy who initially hits it off with Bea. But a misunderstanding leads to their fiery first date turning sour. When they meet again in Australia, he's determined to prove himself and win Bea back.

Supporting Characters:

  • Claudia (Alexandra Shipp): Bea's best friend and confidante. She's fiercely loyal and supportive, offering Bea hilarious advice and encouragement as she navigates the awkward situation with Ben.
  • Leo (Dermot Mulroney): The groom at the Australian wedding. He's a charismatic and easygoing guy who gets caught in the middle of Bea and Ben's charade.
  • Carol (Michelle Hurd): The bride at the Australian wedding. While supportive of Leo, she can be a bit uptight and easily stressed, especially with the addition of unexpected drama from Bea and Ben.
  • Pete (GaTa): A laid-back and funny friend of Ben's who joins him on the trip to Australia. He provides comic relief and serves as Ben's voice of reason amidst the chaos.
  • Halle (Hadley Robinson): Another guest at the wedding who becomes entangled with Bea and Ben's charade. She's a bit quirky and adds an unexpected element to the group dynamic.
  • Roger (Bryan Brown) & Innie (Rachel Griffiths): Leo's parents. They're a warm and welcoming couple who unknowingly add to the comedic situations as Bea and Ben try to maintain their fake relationship.
