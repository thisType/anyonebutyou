After a disastrous first date, two enemies are forced to pretend to be a loved-up couple at a glamorous Australian wedding

Anyone But You is a rom-com with a twist. Bea and Ben have a scorching first date, but things go south leaving them as sworn enemies. Fate, however, has a funny way of working. They find themselves unexpectedly reunited at a destination wedding in Australia. Neither wants to face the social awkwardness of being single at the event, so they hatch a plan: pretend to be a happy couple. Through this hilarious charade, sparks begin to fly once again, but will their past keep them apart, or will this be a chance to rewrite their love story?
