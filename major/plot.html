Plot

Act I: Enemies Down Under

Bea and Ben have a disastrous first date filled with misunderstandings and clashing personalities. Their fiery arguments culminate in a public scene that leaves them sworn enemies.

Bea receives a wedding invitation from a distant relative in Australia. To her horror, she discovers her arch-nemesis, Ben, is also attending the same wedding.

Dreading the social awkwardness of being single at the wedding, Bea desperately seeks a solution. She hatches a plan with her best friend, Claudia, to pretend to be in a happy relationship with another guest.

Act II: The Fake Out

Just as Bea is about to convince someone else to be her pretend boyfriend, Ben approaches her with a similar proposition. They reluctantly agree to fake a relationship for the duration of the wedding festivities.

As Bea and Ben navigate the charade, they start to rediscover the spark they initially felt on their first date. Humorous situations arise as they try to maintain their fake relationship in front of the wedding party, including Bea's supportive but mischievous best friend, Claudia, and the easygoing groom, Leo, who gets caught in the middle.

Despite their growing feelings, past resentments and insecurities resurface, creating tension between Bea and Ben. They face challenges in maintaining their fake relationship, especially while dealing with the bride's uptight personality and a quirky wedding guest who becomes unexpectedly involved in their charade.

Act III: Love Unveiled

A series of comedic mishaps and emotional confrontations force Bea and Ben to confront their true feelings for each other. A public reveal of their fake relationship could lead to utter humiliation, forcing them to decide whether to continue the charade or embrace their newfound connection.

They decide to come clean at the wedding, but their honesty is met with amusement and understanding from the wedding party, who had already suspected their charade.

Bea and Ben officially start a real relationship, realizing that their initial animosity only masked a deeper spark. The movie ends with them enjoying the wedding festivities as a genuine couple, having learned that love can bloom in the most unexpected places.
